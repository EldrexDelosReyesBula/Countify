Security

Data Handling & Protection

We take your privacy and security seriously. This page does not collect any personally identifiable information and does not process any payments directly. All donations are securely handled by PayPal.

Analytics Security

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how the page is used. Data collected is anonymous, encrypted in transit, and stored securely by Google’s Firebase infrastructure. No cookies or personal data are involved.

Platform & Connection

  • HTTPS is enforced for all connections
  • No third-party scripts outside of PayPal and Firebase are used
  • No external trackers, marketing pixels, or profiling technologies are present

Payment Security

Payments are completed through PayPal’s secure infrastructure. We do not store, access, or have visibility into your payment details. All transactions follow PayPal’s security and encryption standards.

Your Control

You may decline analytics tracking at any time via the consent banner. No sensitive data is ever logged or linked to your identity.

Security Best Practices

To stay safe online, always verify that you’re on the correct website before entering any information. We recommend keeping your device software up to date and using a secure internet connection when donating.

Questions?

Analytics & Privacy

Purpose of Analytics

We use anonymous analytics to better understand how visitors interact with this donation page. This information helps us enhance performance, identify usability issues, and ensure a smoother donation experience.

What Data We Collect

  • Page views and time spent
  • Tap or click events on donation buttons or links
  • Basic device type (e.g., mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • App or browser performance (e.g., load times, crashes)

What We Never Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or payment details
  • Precise location (e.g., GPS) or identifiable IP addresses
  • Cookies, tracking pixels, or personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Any data linked to your PayPal account or identity

How It Works

We use Google’s Firebase Analytics, which assigns a temporary, random identifier to each app or session instance. This identifier is used only to generate anonymous usage reports. We do not and cannot use this data to identify you or track personal behavior.

No cookies are used on this page. The analytics are anonymous, secure, and comply with data protection regulations such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Your Control

You are not required to allow analytics tracking. You may choose to decline tracking at any time by selecting "Decline" in the consent prompt. If you’ve previously accepted and would like to opt out later, you can clear this website’s storage from your browser or revisit the consent banner.
